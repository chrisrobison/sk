ABOUT THE BAND

The Suicidal Kennedy's emerged from the underground punk scene with a mission: to channel political frustration into high-energy, infectious punk anthems. Our sound is raw, fast, and unapologetically direct.

Drawing inspiration from classic punk pioneers while addressing today's political chaos, we create music that makes you want to dance, think, and maybe start a revolution.

Currently recording our debut album "Patriot Act Up!" - 17 tracks of pure punk fury that tackle everything from political corruption to social media dystopia.
